1 Introduction

Welcome! This portfolio shows the construction of three different visualizations of the Star Wars universe. The first visualization shows the relationship between average character age and gender. The second visualization reveals how starship classes differ in terms of length. The third visualization shows the distance of Star Warâ€™s planet from their host stars and animates their orbital periods. Planets from our solar system are included for easy comparison.

All the data is from the repurrrsive package. The repurrrsive package includes various datasets to assist in the teaching of R. The Star Wars dataset is originally from SWAPI (e.g., the Star Wars API).
